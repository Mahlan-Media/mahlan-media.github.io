About Mahlan Dircksen

My name is Mahlan Dircksen and I am the owner, founder, and the only person behind Mahlan Media LLC. I started this business to support myself through college while finding a way to give back to the community through creative media.

Around 9 years ago, I received my first drone from my grandma for Christmas. That sparked my passion for flying and aerial cinematography. At the same time, I began creating videos and experimenting with YouTube content before eventually producing videos for family, friends, schools, and organizations.

My experience led me to work with Junior Achievement and later to a digital media short film camp at Indiana University where I trained and developed my filmmaking skills. I later became certified in Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Lightroom, Illustrator, and Real Estate Photo & Video.

Today, I operate Mahlan Media LLC while studying marketing at Purdue Fort Wayne. Every project is filmed, edited, and managed directly by me to ensure high-quality, professional results.
