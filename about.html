About

Professional aerial media with a creative edge.

Indiana Drone Services is a drone-focused brand under Mahlan Media, created to serve businesses, property owners, and organizations across Indiana with polished aerial video and photography.

The approach is simple: create clean, cinematic visuals that help clients market better, communicate clearly, and present their work with confidence.

What to Expect

Quality, consistency, and clear communication.

Every project is handled with professionalism from planning through delivery. Whether the goal is marketing a property, documenting a build, or creating standout brand content, the focus stays on visuals that feel smooth, sharp, and intentional.

Why Clients Choose This
  • FAA certified and fully compliant
  • Clean, stabilized, professional footage
  • Reliable communication and scheduling
  • Fast turnaround when needed
About Mahlan Media

A broader creative foundation behind every project.

Mahlan Media is a creative media company focused on video production, photography, and digital content. Indiana Drone Services exists as a dedicated branch for aerial work, giving clients a specialized drone-focused experience with the support of a wider creative background.
